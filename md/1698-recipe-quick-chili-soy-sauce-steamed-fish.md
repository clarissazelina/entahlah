##### Steps to make Chili Soy Sauce Steamed Fish:

1. Line a steamer with parchment paper and put the fillet in there.
1. Add the bok choy on top of the fish.

1. Mix the water, chili powder, cilantro, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, red pepper flakes, and sugar on a small pan and bring it to boil.
