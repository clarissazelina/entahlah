##### Steps to make Strawberry Mojito #ramadankitayari:

1. Keep all the ingredients ready. Chop the strawberries roughly, chop one lemon into small wedges, the other lemon into 2 for squeezing juice. Take 2 serving glasses (depend how many glasses you make, if you make more double the quantity)
1. Take the chopped strawberries in a bowl, add lemon juice, mint leaves and few teaspoons sugar. Beat well with a spoon.
1. Divide the crushed strawberry mixture into 3 glasses. Add some crushed ice cubes in both the glasses. Add few lemon wedges in both the glasses as well. Shake the glasses slightly to muddle the flavors.
1. Slowly add plain soda or water in all the 3 glasses. Stir and serve strawberry mojito immediately.
